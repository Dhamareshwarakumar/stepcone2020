GO-KART CHAMPIONSHIP

Description

Go-Kart Championship is an event aimed at bringing out innovative and creative potential of young automobile enthusiasts. Undergraduate students from different technical streams can participate in the event. The main objective of this event is to design and fabricate Go-Kart with beauty of practical engineering. The students must function as a team to design, innovate, build, test and compete with a vehicle. All the teams are free to exhibit their creativity as mentioned in the rulebook. The challenge takes place in two rounds one is the statics round and the other is Dynamics round.

    Registration fee per team: Rs.10000/-

    Number of persons per team: 15-25(max)
