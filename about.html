ABOUT STEPCONE

     After being experienced success of being one of the best technical and cultural fests in India. This time GMRIT takes it privilege in announcing the 14 th edition of STEPCONE. This Technical & cultural fest has been on the most awaited platform for engineering students of all streams. With more scrupulous forethought and invulnerable zeal, here comes the latest version which is going to ignite the young engineering minds to unleash their spirit.

     It hosts discourse on theories, problems and their solutions in current revolution of engineering. It showcases the level of excitement which can be visualised when the theories in books are made to compete with others. It enlightens young minds to bring societal impact with highly planned and focused efforts contributed projects. Bashing all the boundaries we are conceiving our advanced version of cultural colloquium bagging marvellously planned events. It is going to build a bridge between the youth of he nation and highly motivating empowerment talks on the other side. A highly advanced technical workshops , a high grade competitive technical events, exciting spot events and challenging spot lights..

     We are forecasting on implacable spirit in students who come together to test their insatiable minds and push themselves beyond their expectations & stand as an inspiration of becoming a responsible youth of this nation enlightening many other minds and soul.
